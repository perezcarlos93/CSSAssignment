Who Am I?

Click below to find out!

I've lived in LA my whole life. I've spent my years at the beaches, mountains, cruising down Hollywood Blvd, and eating at as many restaurants as I can find

I have made a career for myself by working in the event production industry. Organizing, setting up and running sound, lighting, and audio for concerts, corporate meetings, political events, and huge parties has been my job for quite a while. Nevertheless, sometimes change is necessary.

I've been interested in coding for some time now but always found it difficult to learn it on my own. After spending years in the event industry, I decided that the fast pace, unpredictable nature of it all was not something I wanted in my life anymore. I decided to change careers and coding was one of the first things that came to mind. I love the growth that was possible in the developer industry, and I loved the versatility that I could have as well. Ultimately, I loved learning how to code and create new things from scratch, because that's always what I've loved to do; create.
